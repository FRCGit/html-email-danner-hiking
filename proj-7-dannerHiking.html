(Re)Introducing the Free Spirit

The Free Spirit was first introduced in 1988 as part of our innovative Danner Crosshikers™ collection. Three decades later, the Free Spirit returns with updated materials and a modern look that pays homage to its retro hiker roots. It's versatile enough to wear with your daypack or your favorite pair of jeans, making it the ideal choice for adventurous spirits — because your footwear shouldn't hold you back.
